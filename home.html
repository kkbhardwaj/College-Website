Durga Prasad Baljeet Singh (PG) College (DPBS), Bulandshahr

Durga Prasad Baljeet Singh (PG) College is situated in Bulandshahr in Uttar Pradesh state of India. Established in 1915, it is accredited from NAAC and it is affiliated to Chaudhary Charan Singh University. DPBS, Bulandshahr offers 18 courses across 6 streams namely Arts, Physical Education, Science, IT, Commerce & Banking, Education and across 7 degrees like BA, BSc, B.Ed, B.Com, BCA.Hostel facility is not available for its students.

The college provides facilities for both indoor and outdoor games like Table Tennis, Basketball, Badminton, Billiards, Weight Lifting, Volleyball, Football and Cricket etc. Annual games are organized to recruit and promote talented sportspersons. The overall activities are supervised by the sports in charge with the help of a games committee and training in various sports is given by the well-qualified trainers.The growing importance of information and communication technologies in the communication process has necessitated the establishment of this committee. The committee ensures smooth online exchange of information’s with the MHRD of central and state government, Directorate of Higher Education, U.G.C. and other relevant bodies.
